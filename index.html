I received my Ph.D. degree in Artificial Intelligence from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), under the supervision of Prof. Ji Xiang. I obtained my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from the Minzu University of China (MUC) in 2015. During my Ph.D., I mainly researched Technologies of Point-of-Interest Recommendation on Location-based Social Network. My current research interests span location-based social network, recommender systems, information retrieval, computational advertising, knowledge graphs, graph embedding.
